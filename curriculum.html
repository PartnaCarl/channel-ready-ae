How This Is Structured

Each module addresses one dimension of the Sandler Sales System adapted for the indirect channel. Modules build sequentially — the mindset shift comes first, then the specific frameworks, then the repeatable systems. Click any module to open the full interactive version: tabbed panels, role-play scenarios, Sandler frameworks, partner scripts, and checklists.

The Sandler Channel Principle

Every Sandler framework — Pain Funnel, Up-Front Contract, Negative Reverse, DISC, I/R Theory, No-Free-Consulting — applies directly to the indirect channel. The buyer is a partner rep, not an end customer. The consultative posture is the same. The stakes for giving away IP are higher. This program teaches you to treat partner conversations with the same Sandler discipline as a direct enterprise deal.

The Blitz Masters Connection

Channel-Ready AE students are encouraged to complete or enroll in Blitz Masters first. Blitz Masters builds the outbound muscle — call blitz discipline, prospecting volume, accountability habits — that makes Sandler methodology land. You cannot run the Pain Funnel on a partner you haven't reached. Both programs are offered through Partna Company.

Buyer FAQ

Questions a buyer would ask.

Is this a Sandler-licensed program?
No. We've adapted Sandler's publicly documented methodology — Pain Funnel, UFC, NRS, DISC, I/R Theory, No-Free-Consulting — specifically for the indirect channel. The frameworks are Sandler's. The channel application, scenarios, and curriculum are ours. Reviewed by legal counsel.
My partner is Optiv, not CDW. Does this transfer?
The Sandler frameworks are structurally identical across partner types. Whether you work with VARs, MSPs, distributors, or ISVs — the Pain Funnel qualifies the same way, the UFC runs the same way. Scenarios are drawn from multiple partner types, including MSSP, SI, national VAR, and regional reseller.
Do my AEs need Sandler training first?
No prior Sandler training required. Each framework is introduced from first principles and applied immediately to partner situations. Direct-sales Sandler experience is helpful but not a prerequisite. The program is designed to be the first place your AEs encounter Sandler methodology.
What is the Channel Strategy Audit and how does it connect to the curriculum?
The audit is a 2-hour working session ($2,500 flat) where Carl or Natalie diagnoses your partner motion and delivers a written action plan. The action plan maps directly to curriculum modules — so teams that go through an audit know exactly which Phase and Module addresses each gap. It's designed as the top of the funnel, not a standalone product.
What does the certificate mean?
The Channel-Ready AE certificate demonstrates practitioner-level fluency in six Sandler frameworks as applied to the indirect channel. It's a credential that differentiates AEs who can articulate their channel methodology from those who can't — useful in performance reviews, job searches, and conversations with partner leadership about program maturity.
