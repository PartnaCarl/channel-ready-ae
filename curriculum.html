How this curriculum is structured: Each module addresses one dimension of the Sandler Sales System adapted for the indirect channel. Modules build sequentially — the mindset shift comes first, then the specific frameworks, then the repeatable systems. The Blitz Masters outbound program is recommended as pre-work for students who need to build their prospecting volume before applying Sandler methodology to partner conversations.

HOW THIS WORKS
Click any module to open the full interactive version — tabbed panels, role-play scenarios, Sandler frameworks, partner scripts, and checklists. Each module is standalone and links to its own dedicated page.

The Sandler Channel Principle: Every Sandler framework — Pain Funnel, Up-Front Contract, Negative Reverse, DISC, I/R Theory, No-Free-Consulting — applies directly to the indirect channel. The buyer is a partner rep, not an end customer. The consultative posture is the same. The stakes for giving away IP are higher. This program teaches you to treat partner conversations with the same Sandler discipline as a direct enterprise deal.

The Blitz Masters connection: Channel-Ready AE students are encouraged to complete or enroll in Blitz Masters first. Blitz Masters builds the outbound muscle — call blitz discipline, prospecting volume, accountability habits — that makes Sandler methodology land. You cannot run the Pain Funnel on a partner you haven't reached. Both programs are offered through Partna Company.

Buyer FAQ

The Questions a Buyer Would Ask

"Is this a Sandler-licensed program?"
No. We've adapted Sandler's publicly documented methodology — Pain Funnel, UFC, NRS, DISC, I/R Theory, No-Free-Consulting — specifically for the indirect channel. The frameworks are Sandler's. The channel application is ours.
"My partner is Optiv, not CDW. Does this transfer?"
The Sandler frameworks are structurally identical across partner types. Whether you work with VARs, MSPs, distributors, or ISVs, the Pain Funnel qualifies the same way. Scenarios are drawn from multiple partner types.
"Do my AEs need Sandler training first?"
No prior Sandler training required. The program introduces each framework from first principles and applies it immediately to partner situations. Direct-sales Sandler experience is helpful but not a prerequisite.
"What is Blitz Masters and do we need it?"
Blitz Masters is a separate Partna Company program focused on high-intensity outbound prospecting — call blitzes, accountability sprints, volume habits. It's recommended pre-work. If your AEs are already generating sufficient partner conversations, Channel-Ready AE alone is sufficient.
"What does the certificate mean?"
The Channel-Ready AE certificate demonstrates practitioner-level fluency in six Sandler frameworks as applied to the indirect channel. It's a credential that differentiates AEs who can quantify their channel methodology from those who cannot.
