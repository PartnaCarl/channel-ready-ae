Buyer FAQ

The Questions a Startup Buyer Would Ask

"Is the full curriculum recorded when I sign up?"
Phase 1 modules (1–4) will be complete at launch. Phases 2–3 delivered on a rolling SLA. Pilot pricing reflects this. You get a written completion timeline.
"My partner is Optiv, not CDW. Does this transfer?"
The frameworks are structurally identical across VARs. Modules use multi-vertical examples. Team licenses get custom scenarios for your specific product and partners.
"What about the non-compete risk?"
Both founders' employment agreements are reviewed by an attorney before any customer contact. No enrollment until clear. Full refund contingency if a problem surfaces.
"What does the certification mean?"
Practitioner credential — proof of specific skills. No CompTIA/CRN affiliation yet (Year 2). Value comes from the skills and the practitioner credibility behind them.
"How do I know this works?"
6 pilot KPIs measured from Day 1, published after first 6-month cycle. If you're the first buyer, you get pilot pricing and direct founder access in exchange for being the proof point.
