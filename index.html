Since 2023, channel teams have been cut at nearly every mid-market B2B tech company. AEs inherited the partner motion with zero training. This is the program that closes that gap.

The Problem

AEs who inherited the channel motion with no playbook

Your AEs are managing CDW, SHI, Insight, and WWT relationships with instinct — not process. Deal registrations are falling through. QBRs have no structure. Partners are going to competitors who have a program.

The Company Profile

$10M–$75M ARR B2B tech companies

You're growing fast enough that channel matters, but not big enough to have a dedicated CAM for every AE. Your team is closing deals through partners but leaving significant revenue on the table by not enabling them properly.

The AE Profile

Quota-carrying reps managing 5–15 partner relationships

They know how to close direct. They don't know how deal registration actually works, how to structure a partner QBR, how to resolve partner conflict without blowing the relationship, or how to leverage a distributor's resources.

AEs who run the channel motion as a force multiplier

After 6 months, your AEs treat their top 3 partners as an extension of their pipeline — not an administrative burden. Partner-sourced revenue increases. Deal reg quality improves. QBRs become pipeline generators instead of status reports.

AI Agent Add-On

Six agents that do
the operational work for them.

Available to graduates in Month 3+. Six Claude-powered agents connect to your AEs' existing email and CRM — and handle the channel operations work that eats their time.

Agent 01

Email Triage

Classifies and drafts responses to partner emails — deal reg inquiries, enablement requests, pricing questions — so AEs stay responsive without losing their day.

Agent 02

Deal Reg Processor

Validates inbound deal registrations against CRM data, flags conflicts, routes to the right AE, and drafts the approval or escalation response.

Agent 03

Pipeline Tracker

Weekly partner pipeline snapshots pulled from CRM. Flags deals that haven't moved in 21+ days and drafts the follow-up sequence for each.

Agent 04

QBR Prep

Auto-assembles 90-day partner review packages from CRM data — prior quarter performance, open pipeline, next quarter targets — so QBRs run on facts, not memory.

Agent 05

Enablement Distributor

Routes product collateral, competitive battlecards, and training materials to the right partner contacts by tier — automatically, when new content drops.

Agent 06

Conflict Detector

Scans for duplicate deal registrations and territory overlaps before they become partner conflicts. Flags early so AEs can resolve before relationships break.
