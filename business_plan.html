Executive Summary

The one-page version.

Channel-Ready AE is a B2B sales training company that licenses a Sandler Sales methodology program specifically adapted for channel Account Executives. The program teaches quota-carrying AEs at technology companies how to apply the Sandler system — Pain Funnel, Up-Front Contracts, Negative Reverse Selling, DISC, Identity/Role Theory, and No-Free-Consulting — to the indirect channel, where the buyer is a partner rep, not an end customer.

The curriculum is built from practitioner IP: Carl Clendenin's 10+ years running the enterprise VAR ecosystem at Toshiba (CDW, SHI, Insight, WWT, and seven more national VARs), and Natalie Clendenin's channel AE experience at Expel, LastPass, Okta, and CDW. All 12 modules are built. The Blitz Masters outbound program is a companion product recommended as pre-work for students building prospecting habits alongside Sandler methodology.

Pricing: $400 per seat per month. Year 1 target: $108,000. Year 2 with growing enrollment and certificate renewals: $319,000. Break-even at Month 8 from first sale. Gross margin: 88% on training revenue.

WHY NOW
Sandler, Challenger, MEDDIC, and SPIN Selling all teach direct sales. Zero programs exist for the indirect channel — where AEs must qualify partners instead of customers, protect IP from partners who shop it to competitors, and run QBRs that create commitment instead of reporting. The niche is unoccupied. The problem is live at every B2B tech company with a partner ecosystem.
The Problem

Channel AEs are trained to close direct. The indirect model requires a different system.

Technology companies at every stage route revenue through partners — VARs, MSPs, distributors, ISVs. The AEs managing those relationships were trained in direct-sales methodology. They pitch. They give away IP. They build business plans for partners who take the analysis to a lower-priced competitor. They run QBRs that report on the past instead of committing the future.

"The channel AE who gives away free consulting is the channel AE who has no pipeline. The Sandler system fixes this — but no one has adapted it for the channel. Until now."

The Training Gap

Sandler, MEDDIC, Challenger, and SPIN Selling all target direct-sales AEs. The indirect channel has a fundamentally different dynamic: the partner is not the buyer, joint sales require different qualification, and IP protection is a survival skill. Zero programs address this.

The Cost of No-Framework

Channel AEs without a Sandler framework over-invest in unqualified partners, give away technical resources before commitment, and accept non-committal QBRs as normal. Each free consulting engagement is margin bled directly to a competitor.

The Market Tailwind

Post-2022 VC pressure compressed channel teams at nearly every B2B tech company. AEs inherited the partner motion without training. The problem is larger in 2026 than it was in 2022 — and growing.

No Incumbent in the Niche

Channel consulting firms (Spur Reply, JS Group) target large enterprises at $50K+ minimums. Sandler's licensed partners teach direct-sales methodology. The Sandler-for-channel training niche is genuinely unoccupied at the $400/seat price point.

The Product

12 modules. Six Sandler frameworks. One channel-capable AE.

The curriculum adapts six core Sandler frameworks — Identity/Role Theory, Pain Funnel, Up-Front Contracts, Negative Reverse Selling, DISC, and No-Free-Consulting — specifically for the indirect channel. Each module focuses on one dimension of the Sandler system and applies it to real partner situations: VAR conversations, joint sales calls, QBRs, partner onboarding, and competitive displacement.

Phase 1 — Modules 1–4

Sandler Mindset for Channel. I/R Theory, why channel AEs fail, partner qualification using the Pain Funnel, Up-Front Contracts, and Pain Funnel applied to partner conversations. Product-agnostic. Applies identically across VAR, MSP, ISV, distributor.

Phase 2 — Modules 5–8

The Sandler Selling System in Channel. DISC for partner types, Negative Reverse Selling for stalled partners, No-Free-Consulting for IP protection, and the full anatomy of a channel deal with Sandler checkpoints at every stage.

Phase 3 — Modules 9–12

Repeatable Channel Motion. Sandler-based QBRs, joint prospecting through partner ecosystems, competitive intelligence using Sandler posture, and building the operating system so Sandler becomes the default, not the exception.

The Blitz Masters Connection

Blitz Masters is a companion Partna Company program focused on high-intensity outbound prospecting — call blitzes, accountability sprints, prospecting volume. It is recommended as pre-work for Channel-Ready AE students. The Sandler methodology lands harder when students are already generating enough partner conversations to apply it. Students can enroll in both simultaneously or complete Blitz Masters first.

Certificate and Credential

Graduates earn the Channel-Ready AE certificate — a practitioner credential demonstrating fluency in six Sandler frameworks as applied to the indirect channel. Certificate renewal at $299/person every two years creates a recurring revenue stream and maintains program relevance.
