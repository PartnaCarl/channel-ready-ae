Why This Module Exists

Channel sales without metrics is a faith exercise. CFOs don't fund faith.

This module teaches the three-layer KPI stack (leading → middle → lagging), the Win Wire format, and how to present partner performance to your CEO or board. Includes Natalie's leadership reporting framework from Expel — what the C-suite actually needs to see.

Leading — Activity

Deal regs submitted. Trainings completed. Co-sell sessions run. These tell you whether the partnership is active — they don't tell you if it's producing revenue yet.

Middle — Pipeline

Partner-sourced pipeline. Partner-influenced pipeline. Pipeline by tier. Pipeline velocity (days from deal reg to close). These tell you if activity is converting to opportunities.

Lagging — Revenue

Bookings through the partner. Revenue by tier. Revenue per partner rep. Services attach rate. These are the numbers that justify the program's existence.

The Win Wire

A structured partner update. 15 minutes to write. Keeps you on the DM's radar.

Send monthly or bi-weekly. Two paragraphs. No fluff. Format: accounts confirmed, activity completed, pipeline identified, deal regs submitted, what's next.

To: [Partner DM, SM]
Subject: [Your Company] × [Partner] — [Month] Update
Hi [Name], Month [X] update — short version: ACCOUNTS → [X] of [X] co-sell accounts confirmed active ACTIVITY → [X] co-sell sessions completed → [X] deal registrations submitted → [X] customer meetings completed PIPELINE → Total: $[X] ([X]% of quarterly target) → Named commits: [Account 1], [Account 2], [Account 3] → Forward pipeline Q[X+1]: $[X] WHAT'S NEXT → [Specific action 1] → [Specific action 2] Full detail in the shared tracker. Let me know if anything needs attention before our sync on [date]. [Your Name]
Internal Reporting

How to report partner performance to your VP Sales or CRO.

To: [Your VP Sales / CRO]
Subject: [Partner] — Q[X] Channel Performance Summary
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY [Partner] generated $[X] in partner-sourced pipeline and $[X] in closed revenue this quarter. [X] deal regs submitted by [X] active reps. QoQ trend: [up/down/flat]. KEY METRICS → Pipeline (partner-sourced): $[X] ([X]% of target) → Pipeline (partner-influenced): $[X] → Deal regs: [X] submitted, [X] approved, [X] in conflict → Active reps: [X] of [X] trained submitting this quarter → Services attach rate: [X]% TOP 3 OPPORTUNITIES → [Account 1]: $[X], [stage], expected [close date] → [Account 2]: $[X], [stage], expected [close date] → [Account 3]: $[X], [stage], expected [close date] RECOMMENDATION [Continue / Increase investment / Escalate / Consider dedicated channel hire]
Natalie's insight from Expel: "The C-suite needs 3 things: how much pipeline, how much revenue, and whether this is scaling or stalling. Everything else is supporting evidence. Lead with the three numbers."
Operations

Monthly reporting checklist.

Data Collection
Pull deal reg submission count for the month by partner and by rep
Update pipeline tracker: partner-sourced vs. partner-influenced
Calculate services attach rate on closed deals
Review per-rep activity: who submitted, who didn't, what changed
Reporting
Send Win Wire to partner DM
Update internal leadership report (if quarterly, prep the data monthly)
Complete Six Asks RAG scorecard for each partner
