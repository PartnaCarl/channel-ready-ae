Why Training Comes First

You can't supervise what you don't understand.

An AE who has never run a QBR cannot supervise an agent that preps one. An AE who doesn't understand deal reg mechanics cannot review a flagged conflict and make the right call. The program is the prerequisite not as a marketing gate, but because unsupervised agents in channel sales — where partner trust is everything — create more problems than they solve.

THE HONEST STATUS
The agent add-on is a separate product that is not yet built. It requires a technical build (Claude API + n8n + hosted dashboard) and a technical contractor who has not yet been hired. The training curriculum stands alone as a complete product. Evaluate it on its own merits. The agent layer is additive, not required.
What Each Agent Does

Six agents. Each trained on specific curriculum modules.

Agent 1 — Email Triage & Draft

Monitors partner inbound. Classifies intent (deal support, pricing, escalation, general). Drafts contextual responses. Routes complex situations to you for human review. Capacity: 80–120 emails/week.

Trained on: Modules 01, 02, 10

Agent 2 — Deal Reg Processor

Validates submissions against territory rules. Checks for conflicts in CRM. Auto-approves clean submissions within defined thresholds. Routes exceptions with context and recommendation.

Trained on: Modules 04, 10

Agent 3 — Pipeline Tracker

Monitors partner-sourced and partner-influenced pipeline. Flags stalled deals, at-risk opportunities, and unresponsive partners. Generates weekly snapshots without manual CRM pulls.

Trained on: Modules 05, 06, 11

Agent 4 — QBR Prep Package

Assembles 90-day performance data, partner KPIs, trend analysis, talking point suggestions. Formats into a presentation-ready document. Eliminates 4–6 hours of prep per QBR.

Trained on: Modules 09, 11

Agent 5 — Enablement Distributor

Routes the right sales collateral to the right partner contacts at the right time. Personalizes by partner tier and quarterly cord. Tracks asset usage and correlates with deal outcomes.

Trained on: Modules 03, 07, 08

Agent 6 — Conflict Detector

Monitors CRM for territory overlaps and duplicate deal registrations across partners. Flags conflicts in real time with context and suggested resolution options.

Trained on: Modules 04, 10

How It Works

Agents draft. You review and send. Autonomy increases as accuracy proves out.

1MONTH

Full Supervision

Every agent output requires your review before any action is taken. You're learning the agent's patterns, catching errors, and building trust. Expect to edit 30–40% of outputs initially.

2–3MONTHS

Graduated Autonomy

Routine tasks (clean deal reg approvals, weekly pipeline snapshots, standard follow-up emails) can run without review. Complex tasks (conflict resolution, QBR packages, new partner outreach) still require human review.

3+MONTHS

Supervised Independence

Most operational tasks run autonomously. You review a weekly summary of agent actions. Flag anything unusual. The agent has proven its accuracy on your specific partner dynamics. You focus on relationships and strategy.

The dependency on training is not artificial. It's operational. If you cancel the training program, you lose the context needed to evaluate what the agents are doing. The two products are genuinely better together — and we designed it that way intentionally.
